# Bitcoin Average Closing Price Report

## Introduction
This report presents the average closing price of Bitcoin for each year, providing insights into its price trends over time.

## Analysis
The analysis was conducted using a dataset containing Bitcoin price information. The following steps were taken:

1. **Data Preparation**: The 'Timestamp' column was converted to a datetime format to facilitate year extraction.
2. **Year Extraction**: A new column, 'Year', was created to represent the year corresponding to each timestamp.
3. **Average Calculation**: The average closing price for Bitcoin was calculated for each year by grouping the data by the 'Year' column and computing the mean of the 'Close' prices.
